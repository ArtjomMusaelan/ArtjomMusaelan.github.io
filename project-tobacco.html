Project 01

SDG3 Tobacco Mortality Analysis

Data Analyst & Designer 1990-2019 Dataset Strong Correlations r=0.84-0.99 Power BI Dashboard
Overview

Supporting UN health policy decisions through data visualization

Developed comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing global tobacco-related mortality from 1990-2019 to support UN Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Good Health & Well-Being).

Processed and visualized public health data from Our World in Data covering mortality trends across countries and age groups (15-49, 50-69, 70+ years).

The Challenge

Making complex 30-year dataset accessible

Public health organizations needed clear visualization of tobacco impact across global populations to inform policy decisions and awareness campaigns.

The challenge was transforming complex 30-year multi-country dataset into actionable insights for policymakers and public health professionals while quantifying the relationship between consumption and mortality across different age groups, countries, and time periods.

Technical Approach

End-to-end dashboard development process

Data Processing

  • — Processed Our World in Data smoking dataset covering global mortality by country and year (1990-2019)
  • — Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query to standardize country codes and handle missing values
  • — Created proper time series structure with mortality segmented by age categories
  • — Developed DAX measures for dynamic KPI calculations including percentage change and total mortality aggregations

Dashboard Design

  • — Created 4-tab interactive dashboard system with mortality trends, country analysis, and global comparisons
  • — Used stacked area charts for 30-year trends across three age categories
  • — Implemented geographic maps displaying death rates by country with 1990 vs 2019 comparisons
  • — Added scatter plots with regression lines and correlation coefficient displays for age-specific analysis
